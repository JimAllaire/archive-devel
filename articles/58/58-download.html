Communism

By Dorothy Day

Preservation of the Faith, (December 1935):12-13.

Summary: Distinguishes two types of Communism, one voluntarily chosen, a Christian Communism, and the other a dictatorship Communism. Although rejecting an imposed Communism, she also rejects capitalism. Upholding voluntary poverty does mean affirming destitution, and above all does not mean a passive acceptance of oppressive systems. (DDLW #58).
