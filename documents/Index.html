Catholicworker.org Document Repository

This site is a repository of the writings of Dorothy Day in easily readable and repurposable file formats.

Contents:

  • All of Dorothy Day's articles and columns that appeared in The Catholic Worker newspaper from its beginnings in 1933 until her death in 1980.
  • The complete text of four books: The Eleventh Virgin, House of Hospitality, From Union Square to Rome, and On Pilgrimage.
  • Selected articles from other publications.
  • All of the above is also available at The Catholic Worker Movement website.

Using the Repository:

  • You may download articles in three file formats: markdown (MD), PDF, and DOCX.
  • The entire collection may be downloaded in a single ZIP or XXX format.
