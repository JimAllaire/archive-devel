This site is a repository of the writings of Dorothy Day in readable and repurposable file formats.

Contents

  • A complete set of Dorothy Day's articles that appeared in The Catholic Worker newspaper from its beginnings in 1933 until her death in 1980. In all, 721 documents.
  • The complete text of four books: The Eleventh Virgin (1924), From Union Square to Rome (1938), House of Hospitality (1939), and On Pilgrimage (1948).
  • Selected articles from other publications.
  • All of the above is also available at The Catholic Worker Movement website.

Terms of Use

  • The files are presented "as is" and do not represent a critical edition of her texts. The texts were digitized by hand by volunteers and were proofed, but not at a professional level.
